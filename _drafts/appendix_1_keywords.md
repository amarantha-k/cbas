title: Appendix 1 Reserved Keywords and Characters
---
Identifiers starting with the underscore (_) character, must be escaped by enclosing them inside backticks (`).

For example,```SELECT COUNT(`_id`) AS COUNT_EMPLOYEE
    FROM default_shadow;```
